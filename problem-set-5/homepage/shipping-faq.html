Shipping: Frequently Asked Questions


  • How do I make an order?

    • After you know what you would like to purchase, please email or call us to tell us what you would like and we will send you an invoice. Our phone number and email are on our Contact Us page.

  • When will my order be shipped out?

    • Once you place an order we will send you a confirmation email immediately and then we will ship our products after the order has been processed which takes between 1-2 business days.

  • Where do you ship to?

    • We are proud to say we now ship worldwide!

  • Where do you ship from?

    • Our warehouse is San Diego, California where we have been shipping our tea for over 25 years!

  • What are the shipping options?

    • We offer Priority Overnight, Overnight, 1-day, 2-day, Priority, Economoy, and Business Class mail through UPS and USPS. We are also happy to accomodate and work with you to find any alternative shipping method you need.

  • I am looking for a tea but I cant find it on your site?

    • If there is a tea you are looking for that we don't have online, leave us a note on our Contact Us page telling us what you are looking for and we will try to find it for you.
